How do I update Flash Player?

posted on 2014/08/25

To download the latest version of Flash on your desktop or laptop computer, follow the directions below:
 
1. Visit the Adobe Flash Player Support Center
2. Click Get the Latest Version
3. Click Install Now on the next page
4. Follow the instructions from there, based on your operating system and browser

If you use Google Chrome, Adobe Flash Player updates are automatically included in Chrome system updates.
