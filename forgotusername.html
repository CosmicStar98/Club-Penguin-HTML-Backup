What do I do if I've forgotten my username?

posted on 2015/01/09

If you have forgotten your penguin name, please check your activation email. If you and your parent or guardian no longer have this email, click Contact Us below to send us an email so we can locate your account. Remember to include the email address that is associated with your penguin.
