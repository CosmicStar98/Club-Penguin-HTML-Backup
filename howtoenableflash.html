Why is Google Chrome asking me to enable Flash?

posted on 2015/07/14

Google Chrome's recent update disabled their in-browser Flash player. This means you may get an error message that looks like this:
 
 
To fix this, you will need to enable Adobe Flash Player from Chrome's plug-ins. Remember to ask a parent or guardian for help.
 
1. Enter Chrome://plugins into your address bar, then click Enter:



2. Look for Adobe Flash Player in the list that appears:



3. Click Enable for Adobe Flash Player:



 
If you need to update Flash Player, click here for help.
